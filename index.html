A brief overview of Coca-Cola's polar bear advertisements

The first advertisement first took shape in France, in 1922. More ads appeared, over the next 70 years, in newspapers. However, since the spread of the TV, Coca-Cola began showing digital commercials - 27 of them, in 1993. They created their "Always Coca-Cola" campaign, spreading their logo around the world. They used new technology to show this, including computer animations
